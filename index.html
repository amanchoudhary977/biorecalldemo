FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

BioRecall kya sirf biology ke liye hai? +
Haan, BioRecall exclusively NEET biology ke liye design kiya gaya hai. Iska poora focus hai NCERT Class 11 & 12 biology — taki aap ek dum deep mastery build kar sako bina distraction ke.
Kya yeh app dropper students ke liye bhi useful hai? +
Bilkul. Actually, droppers BioRecall se sabse zyada benefit lete hain. Spaced repetition ensure karta hai ki pichli baar padhne ke baad jo weak areas reh gaye, unhe scientifically revise karo — bina sab kuch dobara padhne ke.
Free plan mein kya limits hain? +
Free plan mein 5 chapters and 20 cards per day available hain. Yeh try karne ke liye perfect hai — app ka feel lo, results dekho, fir decide karo upgrade karna hai ya nahi.
Daily kitna time dena hoga? +
15-20 minute per day kaafi hai BioRecall ke saath. App aapko automatically high-priority cards dikhata hai — taki aap limited time mein maximum output le sako. Coaching ke baad, ya raat ko sone se pehle perfect hai.
Kya Android aur iOS dono par available hai? +
Haan, BioRecall Android aur iOS dono par available hai. Plus aap web browser se bhi access kar sakte ho bina kuch download kiye.
Refund policy kya hai? +
